# Code of Conduct

## Standard

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its code of conduct. All participants are expected to uphold this standard.

## Summary

- Be respectful and constructive.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Respect differing viewpoints, experience levels, and institutional affiliations.
- Focus on what is best for the community and for patient safety.

## Clinical and Research Context

This repository supports work in oncology clinical trials. Contributors should be especially mindful of:

- **Patient safety**: Do not advocate for bypassing safety controls, human oversight, or regulatory requirements.
- **Data privacy**: Never share, request, or include patient data, PHI, or PII in issues, pull requests, or discussions.
- **Scientific integrity**: Do not misrepresent benchmark results, fabricate data, or present illustrative figures as measured outcomes.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the repository maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. The maintainer reserves the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct.
